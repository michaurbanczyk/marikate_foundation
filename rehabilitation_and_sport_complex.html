MAIN GOALS of the PROJECT

PROMOTION OF A HEALTHY LIFESTYLE

Thanks to this complex, the development of the city's young generation is activated, high-quality and full post-rehabilitation of military personnel and other sections of the population who suffered during the hostilities is guaranteed, and their return to normal life.

SPORTS EVENTS & COMPETITIONS

Taking into account the high-tech and modern equipment of the sports halls, which will meet international standards, it will be possible to hold various sports competitions at the local, regional and international levels on the basis of the complex.

SELF-SUFFICIENCY

It was calculated that the maintenance of the complex will be carried out at the expense of paid services provided by the rehabilitation and sports complex: rental of premises for holding competitions of various levels (by private structures and individuals), services provided by the complex.

INTERACTIVITY

eSport application for administration and accounting of the Rehabilitation & Sports Complex, coaches, sections, services.

ENERGY EFFICIENCY

Modern architectural and planning, structural, engineering and technical solutions and measures that fully comply with the National Energy Efficiency Action Plan for the period up to 2030 and the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development by the UN.

The rehabilitation and sports

The rehabilitation and sports complex in the city of Vinnytsia will be visited by all groups of citizens:


  • people who suffered psychological and physical injuries (military, IDPs, families of the dead, children, people who were under occupation, people who need psychological support),
  • people with disabilities,
  • pupils of the children's and youth sports school who attend sports sections,
  • physically and socially active youth,
  • elderly people,
  • other citizens.

The following services will be provided in the rehabilitation and sports complex:


  • psychologist,
  • rehabilitation specialist,
  • sports coach.


Next sports sections will work:


  • football,
  • basketball,
  • volleyball,
  • rhythmic gymnastics,
  • taekwondo,
  • volleyball,
  • boxing,
  • gym.

Rehabilitation program features

Effective rehabilitation will be provided by special multidisciplinary teams. They include:


  • doctors of physical and rehabilitation medicine who are responsible for organizing the work of multidisciplinary teams and implementing an individual rehabilitation plan,
  • physical therapists who help patients restore lost motor functions of the body,
  • occupational therapists – specialists who work on restoring people’s everyday, social, work skills after injuries or illnesses,
  • speech and language therapists – specialists who have expertise in communication and swallowing disorders,
  • prosthetists-orthetists who select prostheses, manufacture, adjust and assemble prosthetic and orthopedic products and their elements,
  • psychologists, psychotherapists – help restore a person’s mental health, adjust to the correct perception of the goal of rehabilitation, work on depressive, anxiety, adaptation, acute stress and post-traumatic disorders,
  • medical rehabilitation nurses – work under the guidance of a doctor of physical and rehabilitation medicine, together with physical therapists and occupational therapists according to an individual rehabilitation plan,
  • assistants of physical therapists and occupational therapists – participate in the provision of rehabilitation assistance, in particular, the implementation of an individual rehabilitation plan.

The main task of such a team is the maximum restoration of mental and physical functions of a person. Because it is the person and his needs that are in the center of attention. Both short-term and long-term rehabilitation goals are established and tailored to the individual needs of each patient. First, the multidisciplinary rehabilitation team analyzes them from different angles, and then processes them at the maximum level of their professional capabilities.
